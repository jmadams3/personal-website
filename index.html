Pong AI

Utilizing the NEAT algorithim, I took a crack at creating a game using artificial intelligence. I decided to keep it simple with Pong. With NEAT, my AI will cycle through multiple generations until it finds the best possible one to satisfy pre-determined criteria. The result is a pong game in which you can play against your computer, or which you can watch as you computer challenges itself.
